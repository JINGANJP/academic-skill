## Revision Rules for Academic Tone

- Prefer precise verbs such as `demonstrates`, `suggests`, `indicates`, or `argues`.
- Replace vague intensifiers with specific qualifiers.
- Shorten long sentences when they combine more than one claim.
- Remove unsupported evaluative language.
- Preserve hedging when the evidence is limited.
